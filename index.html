About the Author

I'm still a manager.
Just like you.

I am Sanjay Samuel — a Talent Acquisition Leader at Uber, leading APAC tech and non-tech hiring across India and Japan. 14 years in HR. Teams built from scratch. Two countries. Two languages. And still learning every single day.

My career started at a startup with no HR function and no team — where I built everything from the ground up and grew the company from 0 to 50 people. That experience shaped one belief that never left me: great leadership is about people, not process.

"I did not write this book because I have all the answers. I wrote it because I have been on this pitch long enough to share what has helped me — the good days, the tough ones, the wins, and the dropped catches."

Outside of work — father of two, cricket enthusiast, and someone who believes the best conversations happen over a strong cup of chai.
